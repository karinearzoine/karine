about

I'm a 22 year old Montréal based student who wants to get creative. Thoughout my life, I've been never been adventurous. Therefor, this web site is my way of trying something new, expressing myself and acquire a great deal of knowledge.

  • School

    I'm in my final year as financial mathematics student at Université de Montréal. I have a wide knowledge in pure, actuarial and financial mathematics as well as statistics. I want to make a career by using my knowledge in hope of helping others.

  • My blog on Celiac Disease

    This blog is to allow myself to share with others my experience with gluten. You would be surprised how a misinterpreted diagnosis can affect one's life. Although adopting a gluten-free diet is not an easy process, it is extremely rewarding and life altering.

  • Tutoring

    I'm an experienced tutor in high school mathematics, chemistry and physics as well as cegep level mathematics. Click on the contact me at the end of this page to acquire my services or for further information.

  • Spinning

    After discovering that I had the celiac disease, I decided to take my life into my own hands and started attending spinning class. I instantly fell in love!
