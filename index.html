about

I'm a 22 year old Montreal based student who wants to get creative. This web site is my way of trying something new, expressing myself and acquire a great deal of knowledge.

  • School

    I'm a financial mathematics undergraduate candidate at Université de Montréal. I expect to graduate in May 2016. I have a wide knowledge in pure, actuarial and financial mathematics as well as statistics.

  • My thoughts on the Celiac Disease

    I've decided to put down in writing my thoughts in order to share with others my experience with gluten. You would be surprised how a misinterpreted diagnosis can affect one's life. Although adopting a gluten-free diet is not an easy process, it is extremely rewarding and life altering.

  • Tutoring

    I'm an experienced tutor in high school mathematics, chemistry and physics as well as Cegep level mathematics.

  • Spinning

    After discovering that I had the celiac disease, I decided to take my life into my own hands and started attending spinning class. I instantly fell in love!
